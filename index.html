Get Higher or Lower

Please rotate your phone into landscape mode and then enter fullscreen!

Rules

This is a 2 player card game.
Each round both players draw a card.
Whoever has the lowest number has to smoke and they have to smoke that many hits!
The player who smokes get those hits added onto their score!
To make the game more managable we decided to make the maximum hits 5, so if for example you get a 7 then 2 of your 5 hits must be BIG!
If you get a 9 then 4 of your 5 hits are BIG!
If you are ever unsure who is smoking how many hits, you will see it displayed under the player names.
There are some special cards...
These are the Jack, Queen, and King.
When played against ordinary numbers the Special cards always take the points and NEVER smoke!
Jack - Play T.A.X.I with the word J.A.C.K. Jack steals points.
Queen - If the suits match she smokes, if the don't then the other player smokes. Queen steals points.
King - The other player smokes and their small and bit hits are swapped around :O. King steals points.
King beats Jack. Queen beats Jack.
King vs Queen:
If the suits match then queen has small and big hits reversed for the rest of the game.
If the suits don't match then king has small and big hits reversed for the rest of the game.
